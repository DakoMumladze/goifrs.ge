About us

Goifrs company offers creating seperate and consolidating Financial Statements in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards. If you are a non-financial profit oriented organization registered as a joint stock company or a limited liability company and need IFRS financial statements for loan application, supervisory reporting or other purposes, our highly qualified team will construct IFRS financial statements in a timely manner.
